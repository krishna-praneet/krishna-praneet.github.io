Bio

Krishna Praneet Gudipaty is a Ph.D. student in the Laboratory for Advanced System Software at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. His research broadly spans topics in distributed systems and networking - ML/AI systems, CPS-IoT & sensor networks and Cloud Computing. He holds an M.S. degree in Computer Science from UMass Amherst and B.Tech. degree from the Indian Institute of Technology Madras. He also has 2+ years of experience building production-level, data-intensive and perfomance emphasized full-stack microservices as a software developer at Deskera and Publicis Sapient.
