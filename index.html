About Me

Hello! My name is Krishna Praneet, I am currently pursuing a Master of Science degree in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. In the interest of strengthening my skillset to develop large scale, secure and reliant software, I have taken advanced coursework in distributed and operating systems, software engineering, secure blockchain systems and machine learning.

Prior to my masters, I worked at Deskera for nearly 2 years as a Software Development Engineer. As a full-stack engineer on the integrations team, I worked on the improving the inter-operatability of Deskera applications with various e-commerce channels, point-of-sales devices, payment gateways and shipment tracking systems. I received my Bachelor’s from the prestigious Indian Institute of Technology Madras in Chennai, during the course of which I did two software engineering internships at Publicis Sapient and Boeing.
