About Me

Hello! My name is Krishna Praneet, I am currently working pursuing a Master of Science degree in Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. I’m working towards being an expert in the process of designing and developing large scale, secure and reliant software. As part of achieving this, I am taking advanced coursework in distributed and operating systems, software engineering, secure blockchain systems and machine learning.

My prior experiences have also helped me understand the process of building and analysing large scale software. I worked for 2 years as a Software Development Engineer at Deskera and was involved with full stack development in Java Springboot and React.js. I received my Bachelor’s from the Indian Institute of Technology Madras, Chennai during which I have worked as a Research Intern with Boeing and Software Intern at Publicis Sapient over the summers (2018-19). I also served as a Teaching Assistant for delivering material on computational materials in MATLAB for the course Phase Transformations (Spring 2020).

In the interest of broadening my skillset, during my master’s, I have taken advanced courses in Machine Learning, Quantum information systems, software engineering, distributed and operating systems and in blockchain systems. Over the summer, I’ve been working on developing software libraries for decoding Quantum LDPC codes in Julia and implementing the ideas from my coursework.
